## Industry Blueprints Deployment
**Description**
- Industry Blueprints should be versioned to allow for updates and maintenance.
- Users should be able to install Industry Blueprints with minimal setup effort.
- Industry Blueprints should support customization within the Workflow Designer to meet specific business needs.

**Objective**
- Provide a standardized solution for rapid deployment and customization of AI-driven workflows.
- Enhance scalability and performance while maintaining regulatory compliance.

**Primary Personas**
- Industry Stakeholders
- IT Professionals
- Developers
- Regulatory Compliance Officers

**Secondary Personas**
- End-users of the workflows
- IT managers
- Business analysts

**Design Considerations**
- Scalability and performance optimization
- Regulatory compliance and intellectual property protection
- User interface design and accessibility

**Success Signals**
- Versioned and installed Industry Blueprints are operational
- Customizable workflows meet business requirements
- Performance metrics show improvement over baseline
- Compliance audits indicate adherence to regulations

**Related L2 (from capability map)**
- Versioning
- Installation
- Customization
